About the Expo!

Unlock Your Educational Dreams: Join the Manorama Horizon Education Expo in Doha, Qatar!

Manorama Horizon, the educational portal of Malayala Manorama, India's most widely read local newspaper, is making its inaugural appearance with Educational Expo in Doha, Qatar. This event will showcase India's leading universities, colleges, training institutes, and consultancy services. Don't miss this golden opportunity to engage with key representatives from your desired educational institutions and turn your aspirations into reality.

Robotics Workshops

mmerse yourself in the enchanting realm of Robotics and AI. This workshop serves as a catalyst for young minds to witness their dreams materialize before them. There will be three workshops, each delving into distinct facets of Robotics and AI: the hardware-based Robotic Adventures, and the creative coding aspects of AI and VR.

Quiztival : The festival of Quizzing

An exciting quiz event awaits school students, offering them the chance to compete and win fantastic prizes. Teams consisting of two school students are invited to join the preliminary round scheduled for October 28th. The victors of the preliminary round will advance to the finals and stand a chance to win prizes valued at QAR 2000/-.
